LORD, from the Depths to You I Cry!

1.LORD, from the depths to you I cry!

O Lord, to me give ear

and give attention to my voice;

my cry for mercy hear.


2.If you, O LORD, should mark our sins,

then who, O Lord, could stand?

But pardon can be found with you

that you may fear command.


3.I wait, my soul awaits the LORD;

my hope is in his word.

Yes, more than watchmen wait for dawn,

my soul awaits the Lord.


4.O Isr’el, hope in God the LORD,

for mercy is with him;

with great redemption he will save

his Isr’el from all sin.
