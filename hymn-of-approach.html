Shout to the LORD, All Earth

Shout to the LORD, all earth,

and make a joyful noise!

With gladness serve the LORD your God;

before him lift your voice!

Now know the LORD is God.

Enter his gates with thanks,

Give thanks to him, and bless his name;

Because the LORD is good,

his steadfast love endures;

from age to age, forevermore,
