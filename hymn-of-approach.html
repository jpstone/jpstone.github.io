Psalm 136

O thank the LORD, for he is good,

his love endures forever!

Give thanks unto the God of gods,

his love endures forever!

Give thanks unto the Lord of lords,

his love endures forever!

To him alone who does great works,

his love endures forever!


Praise him whose wisdom made the heav’ns,

his love endures forever!

Who o’er the seas the earth has spread,

his love endures forever!

Who made great lights for day and night,

his love endures forever!

The sun and moon and stars on high,

his love endures forever!


He struck down Egypt’s firstborn sons,

his love endures forever!

And brought out Isr’el’s chosen ones,

his love endures forever!

With outstretched arm and mighty hand,

his love endures forever!

Who split the Sea to form dry land,

his love endures forever!


He brought all Isr’el through the Sea,

his love endures forever!

But drowned the Pharaoh’s company,

his love endures forever!

Who through the desert led his sons,

his love endures forever!

Who killed great kings, the mighty ones,

his love endures forever!


King Sihon of the Amorites,

his love endures forever!

And Og who ruled the Bashanites,

his love endures forever!

On Isr’el he bestowed their lands,

his love endures forever!

His servant’s own inheritance,

his love endures forever!


He saw us in our low estate,

his love endures forever!

And saved us from our foes who hate,

his love endures forever!

Each creature’s food he does supply,

his love endures forever!

Give thanks to God enthroned on high,

his love endures forever!
