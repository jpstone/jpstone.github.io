Hallelujah! Raise, O Raise

1.Hallelujah! Raise, O raise

to the LORD our song of praise!

Praise, O servants of the LORD,

praise the name of God the LORD.


2.From this time and evermore

bless the name of God the LORD.

From the sunrise to its rest,

may the name of God be blest.


3.Far above the nations high,

glorious far above the sky,

who is like the LORD on high,

gazing down on earth and sky?


4.From the dust he lifts the low,

from the ash heap takes the poor,

makes them with the princes stand,

with the princes of the land.


5.He gives barren women sons,

blessing them with happy homes

filled with joyful motherhood.

Hallelujah! Praise the LORD!
